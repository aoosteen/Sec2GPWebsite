OUR PROJECT

The project can show how our cities, lifestyle, and even our opinions on culture and ideologies can change with the development of  technology
We made this model using cardboard, an actual mannequin, fabric, PP flute boards, and paint
We wanted to show that it's not only the physical aspects of our life that can be altered or replaced by technology, but also our more social and cultural parts of life.
We used different tools to make the website and the model. For example LED lights to illustrate the energy generating streets of Japan
