MODEL DETAILS

The project is a symbolic model of what our future could look like as technology advances. This model not only looks at what our city and lifestyle would look like, but also from a cultural and ideological standpoint (because its symbolic :3)
We made this model using cardboard, an actual mannequin, fabric, PP flute boards, and paint
Example text about technology or mechanism.
Our goal for this project is to show that technology might not only influence our lives in the form of shrinking and competitive job markets and the increasing use of AI in schools, but can also affect how we see ourselves in a world where many aspects of our daily life are altered or straight out replaced.
